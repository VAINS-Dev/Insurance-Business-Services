Critical Date

Use: This tool can be used to obtain the critical date of a given policy.

Instructions:

  • Enter a policy number into the policy number field
  • Select Get Critical Date
  • Note, if the system is unable to calculate a critical date, this may be due to a issue on the policy requiring maintenancing actions.
